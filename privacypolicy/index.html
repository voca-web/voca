Children

The Application includes a dedicated "Student Mode" designed for educational purposes, which may be accessed and used by children under the age of 13. The Service Provider is deeply committed to protecting the privacy and safety of children. When utilizing Student Mode, the Application operates entirely on local device storage. The Service Provider does not require account creation, nor does it knowingly collect, track, transmit, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from children under the age of 13. All educational progress, including completed units and vocabulary scores, remains exclusively on the user's device. In the event the Service Provider discovers that any personal information from a child under 13 has been inadvertently collected without verifiable parental consent, the Service Provider will take immediate steps to delete this information from their servers. If you are a parent or guardian and have any concerns regarding your child's use of the Application, please contact the Service Provider at support@voca.com.tr.
